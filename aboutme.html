Climate Change is Killing Animals

More than 1 billion animals have died in the Australia Forest fire last year.

Climate change is real, and is having an effect on the enviorment, such as making wildfires and droughts more common to experience.

As a result of these enviormental conditions, climate change leads to the extinction of about 200 species of animals per day. Thanks to climate change, extinction is happening 1000X faster than before

About .1% of all the animal species go extinct per year.

The causes of climate change

  • Greenhouse gasses trap heat in the earth's atmosphere, warming up everything and effecting it.

Please try these tips to try to conserve the enviorment and keep our animals alive for our generation and future generation.

  • Try to drive less and save gasoline
